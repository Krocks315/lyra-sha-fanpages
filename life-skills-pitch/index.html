Life Skills Hub was created by a licensed notary public. For the youth your program serves — especially those aging out of foster care — document access and legal legitimacy are not small barriers. They are the barrier.

We provide online notarization services directly through the platform. Lease agreements, affidavits, consent forms, employment documents — youth can get documents notarized without navigating a system that was never designed for them.

Grant Eligibility

Your program likely already has a funding path

Life Skills Hub qualifies for funding through multiple federal and local grant programs. If your organization is already receiving these funds, licensing this platform may be covered.

  • John H. Chafee Foster Care Program Primary The federal grant specifically for aging-out foster care independent living
  • Title IV-E Independent Living Funds Federal funds supporting transition services for foster youth
  • TRIO Grants (Federal) For programs serving first-generation and low-income college students
  • Community Development Block Grants HUD-administered funding supporting community service programs
  • Local Workforce Development Funds State and local workforce investment act allocations
