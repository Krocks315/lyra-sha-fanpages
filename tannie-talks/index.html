What Is Tannie Talks?

"Tannie" means auntie — and that's exactly the energy here. Warm. Wise. Direct. No sugarcoating, no judgment. Just a faith-filled woman from New York with Puerto Rican roots, sitting down with you and telling it straight.

Tannie Talks is a talk show, podcast, and community space for faith-based women, BIPOC creators, moms, and entrepreneurial women who are tired of surface-level conversations and ready for something real.

Every episode — Ingrid brings her full self to the table: her faith, her culture, her hard-won wisdom, and her love for the women in this community. This is your auntie's table, and there's always room for you.
