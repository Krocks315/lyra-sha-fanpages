What Makes FocusFlow Different

Four things no other tool offers

FocusFlow isn't just another app. It's a clinically-informed, voice-driven companion that meets children where they are.

🎙️
Tannie Speaks

Tannie is an AI persona powered by Twilio voice integration — she doesn't just display text, she talks to children. Real, warm voice interaction that engages kids who struggle to stay on task through reading alone.

🚫
Zero Shame Framework

Built on a non-punitive philosophy from the ground up. No timers that buzz with disappointment. No punishment loops. Every interaction is framed around progress, encouragement, and the child's own wins.

📱
Any Device — No App Store

FocusFlow is a Progressive Web App (PWA) built on Next.js. It's installable directly from a browser — no IT department approvals, no app store policies, no device restrictions. Works on Chromebooks, tablets, phones, and desktops.

🏗️
Built by Behavioral Health

FocusFlow was created by a professional with direct experience at Magellan Health — one of the nation's largest behavioral health organizations. This isn't a tech product wearing a clinical hat. It's the other way around.
